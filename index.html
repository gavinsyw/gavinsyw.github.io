Biography

About me: I am a Ph.D. student at WiTech Lab of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Swarun Kumar. Before that, I obtained my bachelor's degree (with honor) in Information Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I was advised by Prof. Haiming Jin. Moreover, I have been an intern with Prof. Lili Qiu at The University of Texas at Austin during 2020-2021.

Research: My recent research focuses on internet of things (IoT), wireless sensing and communications systems.
